LIDNS setup

One-time setup, Takes ~2 minutes and Works for ~10.5 hours

Important: Do all steps on your home network or hotspot only. This will not work on school most Wi-Fi as the dns is hard set

1
Download the Certificate

This certificate lets your Chromebook trust our server's HTTPS connections.

Download network-cert.crt
2
Import the Certificate in Chrome
  1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://certificate-manager/localcerts/usercerts
  2. Click Import
  3. Select the downloaded file network-cert.crt
  4. Click Open
3
Change DNS Settings
  1. Press the Search key (magnifying glass) on your keyboard
  2. Type Settings and press Enter
  3. Click Network
  4. Click on your current Wi-Fi network, then click it again to open details
  5. Scroll down and click the Network tab
  6. Select Custom name servers
  7. Enter 192.227.130.71 in the first box, leave all others as 0.0.0.0
You only need to do this once on your home/hotspot network.
4
Pre-warm the Cache

Visit the prewarm tool to pre-load all blocked sites into your Chromebook's filter cache.

Open Prewarm Tool
Select categories and click Start Prewarm.
